Privacy Policy Terms of Use

1. Introduction

Rise Up Kids ("we," "us," or "our") provides an English-learning experience for children, managed through a parent or guardian account. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our mobile apps (iOS and Android), parent web dashboard at app.riseup.kids, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

We comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Children do not create their own login accounts. All child profiles are created and managed by a verified parent or guardian.

Questions about this policy: contact@riseup.kids

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Parent account information

We do not store full credit or debit card numbers. Payment card information is collected and processed directly by Stripe, PagSeguro/PagBank, or PayPal.

  • Name and email address (required for account access and communications)
  • Password (stored using industry-standard hashing; we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Subscription and plan details (plan region, child limit, subscription status)
  • Payment processor identifiers (e.g., Stripe customer or session IDs; PagSeguro or PayPal transaction IDs where used)
  • Brazilian tax ID (CPF) when required for PagSeguro checkout in Brazil
  • Terms acceptance timestamp, IP address, and terms version recorded at checkout
  • Support messages submitted through the in-app contact form

2.2 Child profile information

Children do not provide email addresses or passwords. We do not collect a child's legal full name or date of birth.

  • Display name or nickname (required)
  • Age (optional numeric value)
  • Avatar or profile image (optional)
  • App preferences such as language, theme, and sound settings

2.3 Learning and activity data

  • Course, lesson, and SCORM activity progress, scores, and time spent
  • Video watch history and book reading sessions
  • Stars, badges, and gamification history
  • StarCam mission analytics (such as target word, attempts, and duration — text metadata only, not camera images)

2.4 Audio recordings

When your child completes speaking activities, the app may record audio using the device microphone. Recordings are stored in our secure cloud storage (AWS S3).

Some audio assignment submissions may be reviewed by our team for learning feedback before approval. Chant recordings are stored without admin review.

2.5 Photos and camera use

  • StarCam missions: the app uses the device camera to capture a photo that is sent to our servers for object recognition using Google Cloud Vision. The photo is used only to complete the activity and is not stored on our servers or in cloud storage after processing.
  • Kids Wall: with your child's use of the feature, photos may be uploaded and stored in cloud storage. Posts are reviewed by our team before appearing to other families. Approved posts may display your child's display name, avatar, and age.
  • Profile avatars may be uploaded and stored as part of the child profile.

2.6 Information we do not collect

  • Precise location or GPS data
  • Device contacts, calendar, or SMS
  • Behavioral advertising profiles from third-party ad networks
  • Dedicated mobile analytics SDK data (such as Firebase Analytics or Mixpanel) in our current app builds

3. How We Use Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not use children's personal information for third-party behavioral advertising.

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • Track learning progress and personalize the experience for each child profile
  • Process subscriptions and payments through third-party payment providers
  • Moderate Kids Wall content and review audio assignments when applicable
  • Send transactional emails (such as password reset and account deletion confirmations)
  • Respond to parent support requests
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted service providers to operate the Service. They process data on our behalf and under contractual obligations to protect it:

  • MongoDB — database hosting
  • Amazon Web Services (S3 and CloudFront) — file storage and content delivery for media, audio, and images
  • Google Cloud Vision — temporary StarCam object detection (camera frames are not retained after processing)
  • Stripe — payment processing (United States and other supported regions)
  • PagSeguro / PagBank — payment processing in Brazil
  • PayPal — payment processing where enabled
  • Bunny Stream — embedded educational video playback
  • SMTP email provider — transactional email delivery
  • Flodesk — marketing communications for optional pre-signup lead forms on our website (not in-app child data)

5. Parental Rights and Choices

Because children use the Service under a parent account, parents and guardians can:

  • Review and update parent account information in account settings
  • Create, edit, or deactivate child profiles from the parent dashboard or mobile app
  • Request deletion of a child profile from the mobile app or parent web dashboard (access is revoked immediately; personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days)
  • Request deletion of the entire parent account from account settings in the mobile app or parent web dashboard (access is revoked immediately for the parent and all linked child profiles; personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except records we must retain for legal, tax, or payment compliance)
  • Delete your child's Kids Wall posts where the delete feature is available in the app
  • Contact us at contact@riseup.kids for privacy questions or requests we cannot complete in the app

Billing records required by law or payment processors may be retained separately even after account deletion.

6. COPPA and Parental Consent

We direct the Service to parents and guardians. A parent or guardian must register, subscribe, and create child profiles. By creating a child profile and allowing your child to use the app, you consent to the collection and use of information described in this policy for that child profile.

At checkout we record acceptance of our Terms of Use, including the acceptance time, IP address, and terms version.

If you believe we have collected information from a child without appropriate parental consent, contact us at contact@riseup.kids and we will promptly investigate and delete the information as required.

7. Data Retention

  • Account and child profile data are retained while your account is active.
  • When you request deletion of a child profile or parent account, access is revoked immediately and personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where law or payment compliance requires limited retention.
  • StarCam camera frames are not stored after real-time processing.
  • Audio recordings and Kids Wall media remain stored until deleted through account deletion or applicable in-app delete features.

8. Security

We use HTTPS in production, password hashing, authenticated API access, role-based permissions, and private cloud storage for user media. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we work to protect information using reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures.

9. International Users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from Brazil or other regions, your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. Where applicable, we respect regional privacy rights such as LGPD for Brazilian users.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at https://riseup.kids/privacy and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes may also be communicated by email or in-app notice where appropriate.
